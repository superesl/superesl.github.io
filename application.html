Minimum Qualification

- Citizenship from one of the following: Australia, Canada, Ireland, New Zealand, South Africa, United Kingdom, USA
- 4 year Bachelor’s degree in any field
- Clean criminal background check

Required Documents for an E-2 Visa


- Apostilled copied diploma
- Apostilled National Criminal Background Check
- Valid Passport ID Page
- Resume
- 6 passport sized photos
- Health Statement Form (We will provide you this form)
- Application for Visa (We will provide you this form)
- Signed contract

Interview process


- Submit your resume
- Initial interview with YMesl
- Match you with suitable positions
- Interview with schools
- Review contracts
- Accept position

Visa process


1. Send all your documents (8 total) to YMesl Korea
2. The school submits your docs to the Korean immigration office
3. The immigration office issues your visa issuance number
4. Go to Korean consulate near your place to get a visa with the number
5. Get the Visa (It takes 3-4 weeks)
